About Pro Jirga

Proof-of-work, not paperwork.

Our mission

Give every worker — coder or courier — one honest, verifiable record of what they've actually done, so opportunity stops depending on who you know or what school you attended.

What Pro Jirga does

  • Auto-imported contributions — GitHub commits pulled in the moment you push.
  • Manual proof-of-work — photo, text, or voice log for anything off-platform.
  • One-tap endorsements — peers vouch for you in a single click.
  • Transparent Credibility Score — 0–100, itemized, no black box.
  • Skill challenges — timed 1–5 min tasks that unlock badges.

How to get the most out of it

  1. Log in with GitHub if you code, phone if you don't — both count equally.
  2. Log one proof-of-work a day, even a small one. Streaks compound.
  3. Endorse three peers a week — most send one back.
  4. Attempt one challenge per skill you claim. A badge beats a bullet point.
  5. Share your profile link instead of a CV.
